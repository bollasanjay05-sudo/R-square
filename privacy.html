Privacy Notice

Effective: 19 July 2026 · Last updated: 19 July 2026

We collect only the information needed to answer your enquiry. The website does not create an account, store form submissions in a website database, use advertising trackers or send your information to an AI model.

1. Who is responsible

SIA R Square Consulting and Tourism, registration number 40203584736, with its legal address at “Lubenieki” – 4, Nīgrandes pag., Saldus nov., LV-3898, Latvia, is responsible for the personal information described in this notice.

Privacy contact: info@rsquare.lv

2. Information we collect

The enquiry form asks for only:

  • your name or company name;
  • your e-mail address;
  • your phone number, if you choose to provide it;
  • the service you are interested in; and
  • the message you write.

Please do not enter passport numbers, identity documents, payment-card data, health information or other sensitive information in the website form.

3. How the form works

The website validates the form in your browser. If WhatsApp has been configured, submitting creates a WhatsApp message containing the details you entered. You must then review the message and tap Send in WhatsApp before it is delivered.

Opening or using WhatsApp is subject to WhatsApp’s own privacy terms. Your information may be processed outside the European Economic Area under the safeguards used by that provider. If you do not want to use WhatsApp, contact R Square directly by e-mail or phone.

4. Why we use the information

We use enquiry information to understand your request, respond to you, take steps you request before entering a contract, prevent misuse and maintain necessary business records. The applicable legal basis may be pre-contractual steps, legitimate interests or consent, depending on the request and applicable law.

We do not use website enquiries for automated decisions or model training.

5. Sharing and retention

Enquiry information may be available to authorised R Square staff and service providers needed to communicate with you. It should not be sold or shared for unrelated advertising.

Enquiries should be deleted within 12 months after the last meaningful contact unless they become part of an active client, employee or legal record, or a different retention period is required by law. R Square must implement and periodically verify this deletion process before launch.

6. Website storage and tracking

This version of the website does not use analytics or advertising cookies. It stores only your English/Swedish language preference in your browser’s local storage. You can remove it through your browser settings.

7. Your data-protection rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability or object to certain processing. You may withdraw consent where processing relies on consent.

Send requests to info@rsquare.lv. R Square should verify the requester’s identity without collecting excessive additional information. You may also complain to Latvia’s supervisory authority, the Data State Inspectorate.

8. Security and changes

R Square uses organisational and technical safeguards appropriate to the website’s risk. No online service can guarantee absolute security. This notice may be updated when the website, service providers or legal requirements change; the date above will be revised.
